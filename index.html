DIMENSIONAL CLASSIFICATION: THE COMPLEX

The Complex (commonly known as The Backrooms) is an infinite, non-Euclidean anomaly discovered by ASYNC Corporation. It consists of billions of square miles of repetitive, empty office rooms, characterized by mono-yellow walls, damp carpets, and low-frequency fluorescent humming.

LEVEL 0: THE ENTRYPOINT
The initial zone encountered upon phasing. Structural geometry alters when unobserved. Wireless signals suffer heavy attenuation.

LEVEL 1: INDUSTRIAL INFRASTRUCTURE

THREAT ID: THE WIRE ENTITY (LOG #09)
